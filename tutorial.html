Game Overview

Embark on a thrilling journey of
scientific discovery

AliGence, a groundbreaking 2D science e-learning pet simulation game designed for Grade 7 students at Malabon National Mathematics and Science High School. AliGence combines the excitement of virtual pet care with the intrigue of interactive science education, all guided by a AI chatbot.

Game Features

Virtual Pet Care

Adopt and nurture your own virtual alien pet, each representing a unique scientific concept or principle. Feed, play, and learn about their specific needs to keep them happy and healthy.


AI Chatbot

Acts as a virtual tutor, providing instant answers, explanations and clarifications on various science topics in Grade 7.


Flashcards

Aid in memorization and revision. Students can customize their study sessions, focus on specific topics, and track their progress over time.
